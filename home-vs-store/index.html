Homemade VS Commercial
Baked Goods

Here we present Boxplots and a Wilcoxon rank sum test in order to compare homemade and commercial baked goods

Since, the data was not normally distributed, we performed a Wilcoxon rank sum test to assess wether there was a significant difference between the homemade and commercial baked goods. The difference between the medians of the two sample groups was significant only for the proteins (p-value < 0.05). Whether the baked goods were homemade or commercial, the amount of fats, carbs and fibers were not significantly different (α=0.05).

The table above provides with the 5-number summaries and the boxplots helps to visualize the differences between homemade and commercial baked goods.
